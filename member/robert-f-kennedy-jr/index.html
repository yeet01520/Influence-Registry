About

Robert F. Kennedy Jr. is an environmental lawyer and son of Senator Robert F. Kennedy, who was assassinated in 1968. He founded Children's Health Defense, an anti-vaccine advocacy organization designated as a top spreader of vaccine misinformation by the Center for Countering Digital Hate. He ran as an independent presidential candidate in 2024 before endorsing Trump. Confirmed 52-48 on party lines as HHS Secretary, he oversees the FDA, CDC, NIH, and Medicare/Medicaid. His confirmation drew opposition from medical and public health organizations over his vaccine skepticism, anti-fluoride positions, and claims that cellular phones cause cancer.
